Welcome to B&P Data Consulting.

B&P Data Consulting believes in the power of data-driven decisions and in challenging the status quo. We structure organizations for science practices, creating avenues for more exploration of organizations' potentials and bringing business growth.

We challenge the status quo by restructuring or replacing organizations' data architechture, designing contemporary data instrumentation procedures, reshaping the data collection processes, and tailoring them to meet modern business needs. We wrangle and preprocess both existing and newly collected company data, perform exploratory data analysis using cutting-edge technology, and present results and recommendations in ways that drive key business decisions, culminating in more return on investment. B&P Data Consulting's team of experts builds and delivers quality software, custom, fine-tuned Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI) models, and writes custom scripts that run efficiently (in terms of resource demand and time complexity). We beef up company security by installing AI powered video surveillance systems and networking company's gadgets in secured intranets that meet contemporary network security standards.

B&P Data Consulting is an innovation-driven company that empowers and supports organizations/companies in diverse domains in navigating their digital transformation journery. We also provide specialized trainings on software engineering, data collection and processing methodologies and in developing Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence solutions using state of the art technologies. B&P Data Consulting gives companies a competitive business advantage by ensuring company processes and infrastructure are tailored for science practices. Through our training programs, we inculcate in company employees the ability to drive business growth by making extensive use of business data and data insights. We do not only collect data, explore them, present insights and recommendations in easy to understand ways, but we equally build the capacity of our clients' employees to discover more business avenues from their data with the passage of time.

B&P Data Consulting has extensive expertise in Data Architechture, Software Engineering, Data Analytics, and applies Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence in a collective manner to deliver enterprise solutions that address complex problems including humaniterian crises issues.
